Form Generator

I was inspired by popularized phrases from the reality TV show 'Love Island' to create a form generator, switching between egg-shaped black holes and playful/silly faces. I started thinking about Love Island’s most popularized phrases, and came up with: "HE'S NOT MY TYPE ON PAPER", "DON'T PUT ALL YOUR EGGS IN ONE BASKET", "DON'T MUG ME OFF!", "MY HEAD'S BEEN TURNED","YOU ARE WELL FIT"

‘Don’t put all your eggs in one basket’ has become a spiritual mantra for Love Island followers and for many heterosexual daters - and I am curious about how arbitrary it sounds when decontextualized from the bizarre world of reality TV.I wanted to explore that arbitrariness through randomizing the order of the five phrases (by defining the index then randomizing it).
